Where do I find my wallet address?
  1. Your VeVe OMI / Collect Chain address (starts 0x…).
  2. In the VeVe app → menu → Wallet / OMI (Android shows it directly).
  3. Or the Web Wallet — omi.veve.me → log in → copy the 0x… address (needs a VeVe login; if it won't load, retry or use the app).
  4. Paste it above.

Paste a Collect Chain wallet to scan your real on-chain mints — or click Use sample.

What counts as special (lean): 🥇 THE lowest public mint & 🏁 THE highest (final) mint, the top-5 lowest available, ascending runs starting 0/1 (#123, #1234 — prized above Rare), descending ending in 1 (#321, #4321), 5+ digit palindromes (#12321), repeating digits (#111 / #2222 / #5555 — a lower tier), meme numbers (#69 / #420 / #1337), and historically-significant years (character/grail/easter-egg). The "lowest public mint" uses each item's researched reserve (e.g. #21 when VeVe holds #1–20). Engine: app/assets/mints.js.
"Significant mints" are a collector convention, not an official VeVe designation.
