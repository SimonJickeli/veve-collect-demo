VeVe keeps editions out of collectors' hands three distinct ways: 🔒 held back (reserved low mints, #1 → the lowest public mint), 📦 unsold (still in VeVe's Store), and 🔥 burnt (removed from supply) — asked separately. Anything below a collectible's lowest-public mint is held back for certain; pinpointing the exact owner of a specific circulating edition needs VeVe's own app. Prices are the real VeVe 💠 / StackR ⛓ market floor.
