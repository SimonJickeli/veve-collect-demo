🔗 Built on-chain

Holdings + floors read live from the public collectscan explorer for Collect Chain (VeVe Gem / StackR OMI floors). Read-only, no login, no keys.

How prices work. Your holdings, mint numbers, rarities and editions are read live on-chain, and the MCP math is VeVe's real published formula. Prices use the real market floor — VeVe Gem floor 💎 or live StackR OMI floor ⛓, whichever is cheaper; non-blind items with no floor fall back to their fixed drop price. Blind-box items are shown only when a real market floor exists (a sealed box's price isn't the price of a specific rarity). See wiki/meta/architecture.md.
