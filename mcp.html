MCP Optimizer

The cheapest Master Collector Points for your holdings — ranked by Gems per +1 daily point, using VeVe's real published point formula and factoring your duplicates and near-complete sets.

Paste your wallet for a live read, or use the sample. Your wallet is your VeVe / ImmutableX 0x address — find it in the VeVe app (Account → Wallet) or at omi.veve.me; it's the same address collectscan uses.

How this works. Δ MCP/day is exact (VeVe's published formula: rarity bonuses, low-mint, duplicate diminishing, set-completion). "💎 / +1 day" = cost ÷ daily points gained — lower is cheaper. Cost is the live StackR on-chain floor ⛓ (StackrListings on Base → OMI→USD) where an item is currently listed, else its original drop price ~. Rank/level projections are intentionally omitted because VeVe doesn't publish exact thresholds. Source: wiki/sources/economy-gems-omi-mcp.md.
