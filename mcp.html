The cheapest Master Collector Points for your holdings — ranked by Gems per +1 daily point, using VeVe's real published point formula and factoring your duplicates and near-complete sets.

Where do I find my wallet address?
  1. It's your VeVe Collect Chain (OMI) address — starts 0x…, 42 characters. VeVe manages it for you (self-custody isn't live yet), so you only need to read it.
  2. Web wallet — most reliable: open veve.me/wallet → log in with your VeVe account → copy the 0x… address. (This is where omi.veve.me now redirects — the old link is retired. Needs your VeVe login; if it won't load, try incognito or another browser.)
  3. Or from collectscan: if your wallet page is already open on collectscan.com, the 0x… in the address bar is it.
  4. Paste it above — we remember it on this device, so you only enter it once. Coming: connect StackR to auto-fill.

Enter your wallet for personalised results (ranking factors your holdings & duplicates), or use the sample. Your wallet is your VeVe Collect Chain 0x address — see “Where do I find my wallet address?” above. Entered once, it's remembered on this device.

How it works. MCP/day uses VeVe's published formula (rarity, low-mint, duplicate diminishing, set completion). 💎/point = cost ÷ points added — lower is cheaper, priced at the cheaper of the StackR ⛓ traded floor or a sanitized VeVe 💠 ask. Blind-box items are priced from a real secondary-market floor only — never the sealed-box price. Not financial advice.
